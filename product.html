Teas grown in Darjeeling

Black Tea

Darjeeling black tea is the most well-known and popular type of tea from the region. It is grown in the high-altitude tea gardens of Darjeeling and has a distinctive floral aroma and musky spiciness.

$15.00

Green Tea

Darjeeling green tea is a relatively new addition to the region, but it has quickly gained popularity among tea enthusiasts. It has a delicate flavor and a light golden color, and is rich in antioxidants.

$15.00

White Tea

Darjeeling white tea is the most delicate and rarest type of tea produced in the region. It is made from the young, unopened leaves of the tea plant, and has a subtle flavor with a sweet, floral aroma.

$18.00

Oolong Tea

Darjeeling oolong tea is a partially oxidized tea that has a unique flavor profile that falls somewhere between black and green tea. It is known for its complex flavor, floral aroma, and smooth texture.

$18.00

Autumn Flush Tea

Darjeeling also produces a special type of tea called "autumn flush" tea. This tea is made from the leaves that are harvested during the autumn months, and has a fuller flavor and darker color than other types of Darjeeling tea.
